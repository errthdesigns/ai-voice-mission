## ⚡ Quick Start for Clients

### **Simplest Method:**
1. **Extract** this folder to desktop
2. **Double-click** `start-game.sh` (Mac) or `start-game.bat` (Windows)
3. **Say "PLAY"** when browser opens

### **Demo Time:** 5-7 minutes
### **Setup Time:** 2 minutes
